Our Story

Ụlọ Ọma was born from personal frustration with the traditional rental process in Nigeria. Our founders experienced firsthand the challenges of finding quality housing - from dealing with unreliable agents to paying excessive fees for substandard services.

We realized that technology could solve these problems by creating direct connections between property owners and renters, eliminating unnecessary middlemen and creating a more efficient, transparent marketplace.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of users across Nigeria, helping them find homes and list properties with confidence and ease.
